LOW-COST TEXTBOOKS

Planet eBook

  • Find classic literature as free downloads
  • Adds new titles at regular intervals
  • Subscribe to the Planet eBook blog or newsletter for updates
  • Use previews to get an overview without committing

E-Books Directory

  • Free downloadable texts, documents, and lecture notes
  • Contains nearly 1700 titles
  • Search directory by keyword or browse hundreds of categories

Science Books Online

  • Directory of free e-books for all areas of science
  • Books range from small PDF pamphlets to full-length texts
  • Most must be downloaded but some may be viewed directly within browser

Free Book-s

  • Search engine that scans e-books to find free content

BookServer

  • Search engine for ebooks that are both free and for sale

Flat World Knowledge

  • Free textbooks by experts in various academic fields

Google Books

  • Thousands of public domain books that can be viewed and downloaded for free
  • Go to advanced search options and select "public domain only" and "full text" options

Open Educational Resources - Community College Consortium

  • Open and free texts
  • For descriptions, see listings in MERLOT and OER Commons
