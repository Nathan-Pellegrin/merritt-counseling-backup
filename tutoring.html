RESOURCES FOR FREE
and/or
LOW-COST TUTORING

Khan Academy

  • Free, user-friendly Math and Physics video tutorials
  • Hands-on, step-by-step learning working alongside tutor
  • Math program presents problems that grow more difficult as students progress
  • Widely used and praised by students and educators

VirtualNerd

  • Hundreds of online videos guide students through Math and Physics concepts such as expressions, polynomials and factoring a trinomial
  • Gives students the option to zero in on a concept they are struggling with
  • Limited free account (for Algebra I only)
  • Full subscription is relatively inexpensive: about $40/month
